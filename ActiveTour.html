Overview


Welcome to the Big Tipper Tournament!


Key Details


  • Maps: Map1, Map2, Map3

  • Max Teams: 16

  • Prize Pool: $1000


Tournament Format


  • Top 256 - Top 32: Bo1 of BO3

  • Top 16 - Semi Final: BO3 of BO3

  • Final: BO5 of BO3 in Power League

  • Hypercharges are authorized!

  • New Brawler ban!


Modes and Maps


  • Modes and maps are randomly selected for each set from the list:

    • Set 1: Gem Grab or Bounty

    • Set 2: Brawl Ball or Knockout

    • Set 3: Heist or Hot Zone

    • Set 4: Bounty, Heist, or Brawl Ball

    • Set 5: Gem Grab, Hot Zone, or Knockout

  • Maps are chosen at random from the 3 competitive maps.


Rewards


  • 100% of the Cash Prize for the winning team.

  • If the cash prize exceeds $100 with donations, distribution will be:
    • 70% for the first place.

    • 30% for the second place.

Rules



Here are the rules for the Big Tipper Tournament:


  1. Teams must consist of 3 players.

  2. All matches will be played on the specified maps.

  3. Cheating or exploiting bugs will result in disqualification.

  4. Respect all players and organizers.

  5. 5-minute delay tolerance for match start. Incomplete teams lose the match.

  6. Connection issues or crashes are your responsibility (except for game bugs).

  7. No room hopping! Admins may sanction with a lost match.


Registration


  • Register with a team for bracket placement or waitlist.

  • All team members must be present at the start time.

  • Team invites must be accepted to count.


Check In


  • Check in before the tournament start time.

  • Failure to check in may disqualify your team.


Bans


  • Power League: 3 Brawler bans per team.

  • Normal format: Optional double-blind bans (2 Brawlers).


Matches


  • Match invites should be created by a player in the lobby. Ensure:

    • Player sends invite link to teammates and opponents.

    • Exit unrelated games/lobbies.

    • Disable Do Not Disturb and unmute notifications.



Results


  • Results are recorded automatically by our team.

Any Issues?

  • Contact admins via Discord by creating a ticket.
